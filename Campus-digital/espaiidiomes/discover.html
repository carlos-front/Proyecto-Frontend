ENGLISH TODAY

English is in vogue and its knowledge has become a requirement for a number of positions.English is the language that rules the world; just take a look at the Internet, the broader information source than mankind could ever have imagined, approximately 80% of the information is in English.

It is estimated that over 400 million people have English as their mother tongue and the number grows if we take as a reference countries where English is considered as a second language. Although English is not official in most countries, it is currently the most frequently studied second language.

English is one of the five official languages ​​of the United Nations and also in many other international organizations, such as the International Olympic Committee. If we refer to technology and computer science, most of the terminology or specialized vocabulary is in English and, by international treaty, it is the official language of aeronautical and maritime communications.

But the area where English is more relevant is in the business world. In recent years, globalization has generated enormous changes in the economy, business and the labour market. As our environment becomes more global, the proficiency of a second language becomes more necessary to successfully meet the challenges demanded by the economic and professional activity on the 21st century.

Most companies nowadays require English to access new positions. English can be the difference between closing a deal or not; have a better salary or Access to new job opportunities; successfully position a product or service in the world market. Organizations and countries that have designed and carried out the globalization process have English as their mother tongue or working language. Therefore, whenever you want to do business with a company in another country where both languages ​​are different, English will be the used language.

Globalization brings us to the reality of using a second language and English is the most widely accepted option When developing a website for any company that wants to compete in a global economy, information must also be in English. Thus, the image of the company will acquire international recognition and the product or service will be better positioned.

In conclusion, our society must be aware that English proficiency is one of the most useful and necessary tools for establishing international business.
